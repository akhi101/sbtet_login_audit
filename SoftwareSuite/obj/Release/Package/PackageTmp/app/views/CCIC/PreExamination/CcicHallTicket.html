Instructions :
1. Hall-ticket issued to you is an important document preserve carefully upto declaration of the final result.
2. No candidate will be allowed to enter the examination hall without proper hall-ticket
3. Candidate shall arrive at the examination center at least 30 minutes before the commencement of the examination. While entering the examination hall every student shall deposit all books, personal belongings etc at counter provided.
4. No printed / written material, in any form, shall be taken inside the exam hall, other than hall ticket.
5. Every student shall cooperate while pockets are being checked or frisked.
6. Candidates will not be allowed to enter the examination hall, after half-an-hour of the commencement of examination.
7. Candidates will not be allowed to leave the examination hall before one hour from the commencement of examination. After one hour, if any candidate wishes to leave or wants to attend any emergency needs, the candidate must return his / her answer script along with question paper to the Invigilator.
8. Candidates are advised to go through the instructions given on Answer Booklet or OMR Bar Code Sheet before starting answering.
9. Candidates should not write any matter inside the Answer Booklet which may lead to the identification of the Candidate or institution. If he /she do so, he / she will be booked under malpractice. No color sketch pens are to be used unless specified question.
10.Candidates should carry their own Scientific Calculators, Pens, Pencils and required drawing instruments.
11.Candidates will not be allowed with Cell Phones, Organizers, PDA’s and palmtops or any other Electronic Gadgets, etc.
12.Every student shall follow the regulations during examinations, failing which he / she will be booked under malpractice case.
13.Candidates are advised to check all the pages in the ’16/24-page Answer Booklet’ supplied to him / her. All answers are to be written within the given booklet only.
14.No additional sheets will be supplied under any circumstances.Do Not write any matter on the Question Paper, to avoid malpractice.
15.Candidates Are Requested to crosscheck the Examination Dates with time-table Dates and attend Examination as per the time-table.
